Pokémon Music Mixer

About

Welcome to the **Pokémon Music Mixer**! This is a fan-made web application designed for Pokémon enthusiasts and aspiring music producers alike.

Drag and drop your favorite Pokémon sounds from the **Sound Dex** into the **Sequence Slots** to compose unique battle anthems, route music, or just fun jingles.

Created with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using the Web Audio API, this project aims to provide a simple yet engaging way to experiment with sound sequencing in a familiar and beloved world.

Got a great sequence? Challenge a friend to a sound battle!

How to Use the Pokémon Music Mixer

  1. **Drag & Drop:** From the "Sound Dex" above, drag any Pokémon sound into one of the six "Sequence Slots."
  2. **Fill All Slots:** You need to fill all 6 slots to activate the playback controls.
  3. **Play Battle!:** Click the "Play Battle!" button to start your custom Pokémon groove!
  4. **Controls:**
    • `▶ Play Battle!`: Starts playback from the current position.
    • `■ Stop Battle!`: Stops playback and resets the current position.
    • `⏮ Rewind`: Jumps to the previous sound in the sequence.
    • `⏭ Fast Forward`: Jumps to the next sound in the sequence.
    • `Clear Sequence`: Empties all slots and stops playback.
  5. **Master Volume:** Adjust the slider at the bottom to control the overall volume.
  6. **Remove Sounds:** Click the 'x' button on any filled slot to remove that sound.
  7. **Enjoy:** Create your own epic Pokémon sound battles and memorable tunes!

Note: Ensure your browser allows audio playback; you may need to click the play button once to enable the audio context.
